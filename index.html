Fédération Européenne de la Formulaire Invitations

FEFI unites four European publishers who promote France Passion’s “formule invitations” in their respective countries. The founder and pioneer since 1993, France Passion offers probably the most authentic way for motorhomers to experience a country.

What is “la formule invitations”?

  • Stay on exclusive private grounds
  • Meet local producers and enjoy rural pubs
  • 24 h free stopover with the national annual windscreen sticker
  • Buy fresh and tasty food on site

What is FEFI?

Travelling suggests crossing borders – and for motorhomers “la formule invitations” has already done so. We want to show you how easy and convenient this way of travelling is, and invite you to experience the kind hospitality and culinary diversity of our neighbouring countries.
